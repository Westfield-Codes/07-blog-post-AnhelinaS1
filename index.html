The 3 Kinds of Turkies

The "best" place in Turkey depends on your interests…

10/08/2025 by Anhelina Streliana

For a first-time visit, go to Istanbul for history and culture, Cappadocia for unique landscapes and hot air balloons, and the Turkish Riviera (Antalya/Fethiye/Bodrum) for beaches and ancient ruins are excellent choices. Let's see what kind of vacation you would prefer.

You looking for History & Culture:

  • Istanbul: The cultural heart of Turkey, visit the Hagia Sophia and Blue Mosque, explore the Grand Bazaar, and experience the vibrant city life.
  • Cappadocia: Explore ancient cave churches and underground cities, and take a hot air balloon ride for breathtaking views of the unique rock formations.
  • Ephesus: Discover one of the best-preserved ancient Roman cities in the world, featuring the ruins of the Temple of Artemis.

You like Beach & Nature:

  • Antalya & the Turquoise Coast:A beautiful stretch of coastline offering stunning beaches, ancient ruins likePhaselis and Patara, and charming seaside towns.
  • Fethiye: Base yourself here to explore the coast, including the nearby Ölüdeniz, and the ancient rock-cut tombs of Myra.
  • Dalyan: Known for its unique therapeutic mud baths and a vital nesting ground for Loggerhead sea turtles at Iztuzu Beach.
  • Bodrum: a lively Turkish resort town known for its beautiful beaches, vibrant nightlife, whitewashed buildings, bustling bazaars, and a blend of ancient history and modern luxury, drawing tourists for its sunny climate, water activities, sailing, and luxurious atmosphere.

You looking for Unique Experiences:

  • Cappadocia's Hot Air Balloons: A world-renowned experience, offering stunning sunrise views over the "fairy chimney" rock formations.
  • Dalyan Mud Baths: A unique and rejuvenating experience where you cover yourself in mineral-rich mud and then rinse off in hot springs.
